# ETF Flows Pipeline Placeholder

This directory is reserved for ETF flow inputs used by the passive market instability research notebook.

The first notebook version uses `volume * return` as a weak flow-pressure proxy because official ETF creations, redemptions, or shares-outstanding changes may not be available locally.

Place raw ETF flow, shares outstanding, or price-volume files under `data/raw/` and cleaned research tables under `data/processed/`.
